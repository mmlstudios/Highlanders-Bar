Welcome to Highlanders Bar

About

No TV’s – No Loud Music – Just Talk.

We offer the type of friendly atmosphere that you know from english/scottish pubs both at home and around the world, many off our staff are from scotland & england, whilst others are locals.

We have a selection of more than 200 different whiskies, 100 different rums, around 20-30 different bourbon & rye whisky, 16 beer taps which change freqeuently and have recently introduced around 10 different gins.

Booking

If you would like to organise a private tasting, whether it is for a group of friends, a club or for your company we can organise this for you. It can be based on our ‘a la carte’ tasting menu or we can organise something special.

Our prices vary depending on various factors. If you are on a budget or want to really splash out then we can usually figure something out.
